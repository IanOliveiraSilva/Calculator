Calculate your BMR

BMR is calculated using the formula: activity rate factor x {66 + [(13.7 x Weight(kg)) + ( 5 x Height(cm)) – (6.8 x Age(years))]}

Basal metabolic rate (BMR) is the amount of energy needed to maintain the body's vital functions over a 24-hour period. It is measured in calories, which is the energy extracted by our body from macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins and total fats).
